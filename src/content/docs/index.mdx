## About this wiki

This wiki surveys the empirical and theoretical research on **voter education** and its
relationship to two outcomes that are often conflated but are analytically distinct:
*whether* people vote (turnout) and *how well* the resulting votes reflect voters'
underlying preferences (representation). Educating voters and turning them out are not the
same goal, and interventions that succeed at one do not always succeed at the other.
