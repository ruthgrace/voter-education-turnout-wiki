> Juelich, Courtney L., and Joseph A. Coll. 2021. "Ranked Choice Voting and Youth Voter Turnout: The Roles of Campaign Civility and Candidate Contact." Politics and Governance 9(2): 319–331.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.17645/pag.v9i2.3914) · Published 2021

Uses survey data across RCV and non-RCV US localities to test mechanisms linking RCV to youth turnout. Finds younger voters more likely to vote in RCV cities, with increased candidate contact explaining more of the turnout boost than perceived campaign civility.
