> Lijphart, Arend. 2012 [1999]. Patterns of Democracy: Government Forms and Performance in Thirty-Six Countries, 2nd ed. New Haven, CT: Yale University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://yalebooks.yale.edu/book/9780300172027/patterns-of-democracy/) · Published 2012

Classifies 36 democracies (1945–2010) along majoritarian vs. consensus dimensions, with PR central to the consensus model. Argues consensus/PR democracies match majoritarian ones on economic management while outperforming them on "kinder, gentler" outcomes — higher turnout, better women's representation, and closer government–voter policy proximity. (Scholarly monograph.)
