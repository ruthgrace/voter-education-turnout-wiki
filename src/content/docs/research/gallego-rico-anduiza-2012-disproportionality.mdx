> Gallego, Aina, Guillem Rico, and Eva Anduiza. 2012. "Disproportionality and Voter Turnout in New and Old Democracies." Electoral Studies 31(1): 159–169.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.electstud.2011.11.007) · Published 2012

Cross-national analysis distinguishing established from newer democracies. Finds electoral disproportionality (the opposite of PR) depresses turnout in old democracies but not in new ones, with the negative relationship strengthening as democracies age — driven by small-party mobilization and a growing turnout gap between small- and large-party supporters.
