> Pettigrew, Stephen, and Dylan Radley. 2025. "Overvotes, Overranks, and Skips: Mismarked and Rejected Votes in Ranked Choice Voting." Political Behavior (online first).

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11109-025-10028-4) · Published 2025

Analyzes ~3 million cast-vote records from RCV jurisdictions including San Francisco (plus Alaska, Maine, New York), classifying ballot errors as overvotes, overranks, and skips. About 4.8% of voters mismark their ballot in at least one way, and RCV votes are roughly 10 times more likely to be rejected for improper marks than non-RCV votes, with higher mismarking in areas with more racial minorities, lower income, and lower education. (Online first.)
