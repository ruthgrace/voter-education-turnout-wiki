> Singh, Shane P. 2021. Beyond Turnout: How Compulsory Voting Shapes Citizens and Political Parties. Oxford: Oxford University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1093/oso/9780198832928.001.0001) · Published 2021

Comprehensive treatment showing compulsory voting's effects reach "beyond turnout": compelled voters are less likely to base choices on ideology or issue positions, satisfaction with democracy is altered, and parties shift toward programmatic (away from clientelistic) strategies. Argues mandatory voting reshapes citizen attitudes and vote choice, not just participation rates.
