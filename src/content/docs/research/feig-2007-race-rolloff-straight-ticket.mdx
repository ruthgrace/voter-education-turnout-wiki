> Feig, Douglas G. 2007. "Race, Roll-Off, and the Straight-Ticket Option." Politics & Policy 35(3): 548–568.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1747-1346.2007.00074.x) · Published 2007

Analyzes how the straight-ticket ballot option and voter race relate to roll-off. Finds ballot-design features (the straight-party device) and demographic factors jointly shape completion rates, reinforcing that roll-off is driven by both ballot mechanics and the information/effort demands placed on different voters.
