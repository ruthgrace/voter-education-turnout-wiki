> Galston, William A. 2001. "Political Knowledge, Political Engagement, and Civic Education." Annual Review of Political Science 4: 217–234.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev.polisci.4.1.217) · Published 2001

A foundational review arguing that political knowledge matters (it shapes acceptance of democratic norms, issue attitudes, and participation) yet has stagnated despite rising formal education. Concludes that traditional classroom civic education can significantly raise political knowledge and that service learning is a promising complement.
