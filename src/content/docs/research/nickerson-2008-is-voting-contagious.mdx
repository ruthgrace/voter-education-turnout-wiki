> Nickerson, David W. 2008. "Is Voting Contagious? Evidence from Two Field Experiments." American Political Science Review 102(1): 49–57.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1017/S0003055408080039) · Published 2008

Uses a placebo-controlled door-to-door design in two-voter households, where the answerer received either a GOTV or a recycling (placebo) pitch, to measure within-household spillover. About 60% of the turnout effect of canvassing was passed to the other household member, demonstrating contagion of voting behavior.
