> Green, Donald P., and Alan S. Gerber. Get Out the Vote: How to Increase Voter Turnout. Washington, DC: Brookings Institution Press. (1st ed. 2004; later editions 2008, 2015, 2019, 2024.)

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://www.brookings.edu/books/get-out-the-vote-2/) · Published 2019

The authoritative practitioner-oriented synthesis of GOTV field-experiment evidence, translating dozens of randomized studies into cost-per-vote guidance across canvassing, mail, phone, email, and text. It ranks door-to-door canvassing and personal contact as most effective and reports comparative cost-effectiveness for each tactic.
