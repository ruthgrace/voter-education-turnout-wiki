> McDaniel, Jason A. 2016. "Writing the Rules to Rank the Candidates: Examining the Impact of Instant-Runoff Voting on Racial Group Turnout in San Francisco Mayoral Elections." Journal of Urban Affairs 38(3): 387–408.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/juaf.12209) · Published 2016

Uses aggregate precinct-level racial-group data across five San Francisco mayoral elections (1995–2011) to estimate IRV's effect on turnout. Finds IRV adoption associated with declines in Black turnout (~18%) and White turnout (~16%) relative to pre-IRV contests, with no significant change for Asian or Latino participation.
