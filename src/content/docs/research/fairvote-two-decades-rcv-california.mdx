> Reisman, Avram. "Two Decades of Ranked Choice Voting in California." Washington, DC: FairVote (advocacy-organization report; originally ~2023, updated edition on FairVote's site).

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://fairvote.org/report/two-decades-of-rcv-in-california/) · Published 2023

A descriptive report on California's four single-winner RCV cities — San Francisco (2004), Oakland, Berkeley, and San Leandro (2010). Reports large participation gains in decisive elections (e.g., Oakland mayoral participation up ~50% moving from a low-turnout primary to the RCV general) and representation gains (candidates of color won 64% of RCV races vs. 36% before). (Advocacy-organization report — not peer-reviewed.)
