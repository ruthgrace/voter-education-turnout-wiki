> Bergman, Elizabeth, and Philip A. Yates. 2011. "Changing Election Methods: How Does Mandated Vote-By-Mail Affect Individual Registrants?" Election Law Journal 10(2): 115–127.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1089/elj.2010.0079) · Published 2011

Exploits a California quasi-experiment in which small precincts are converted by law to mail-only balloting, comparing individual registrants' turnout before and after conversion. Finds heterogeneous effects: mandated VBM tended to sustain participation among habitual voters but did little to mobilize (and in places dampened turnout among) lower-propensity registrants, implying small net turnout effects.
