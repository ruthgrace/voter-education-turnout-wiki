> Dale, Allison, and Aaron Strauss. 2009. "Don't Forget to Vote: Text Message Reminders as a Mobilization Tool." American Journal of Political Science 53(4): 787–804.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-5907.2009.00401.x) · Published 2009

A nationwide randomized field experiment (n ≈ 8,053) delivering Election Day SMS reminders in 2006. Text reminders raised turnout by about 3.0 percentage points at very low cost, and the authors argue impersonal but noticeable reminders can mobilize without social connectedness (a "noticeable reminder" theory).
