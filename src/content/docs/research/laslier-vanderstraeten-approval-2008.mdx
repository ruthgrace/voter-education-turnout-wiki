> Laslier, Jean-François, and Karine Van der Straeten. 2008. "A Live Experiment on Approval Voting." Experimental Economics 11(1): 97–105.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10683-006-9149-6) · Published 2008

An in-situ field experiment run alongside the real 2002 French presidential first round, in which several thousand actual voters also cast a parallel approval ballot. Finds approval voting feasible, well accepted, and easily understood; voters approved multiple candidates and the resulting ranking differed from the official plurality ranking, with extreme candidates faring relatively worse under approval voting.
