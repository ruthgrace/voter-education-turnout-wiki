> Blais, André, and R. Kenneth Carty. 1990. "Does Proportional Representation Foster Voter Turnout?" European Journal of Political Research 18(2): 167–181.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1475-6765.1990.tb00227.x) · Published 1990

Regression analysis of 509 national elections in 20 democracies. Finds turnout is systematically higher under PR than under plurality/majority systems — on the order of several percentage points — and that this gap persists after controlling for compulsory voting, competitiveness, and other factors.
