> Amy, Douglas J. 2002. Real Choices/New Voices: How Proportional Representation Elections Could Revitalize American Democracy, 2nd ed. New York: Columbia University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://cup.columbia.edu/book/real-choices-new-voices/9780231125499/) · Published 2002

The canonical US-focused scholarly case for proportional representation (including STV / "choice voting"), arguing PR would improve fair representation for third parties, women, and racial minorities, reduce wasted votes and spoilers, and better align policy with public preferences. (Academic-press monograph — not a peer-reviewed article.)
