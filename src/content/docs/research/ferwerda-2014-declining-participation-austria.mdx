> Ferwerda, Jeremy. 2014. "Electoral Consequences of Declining Participation: A Natural Experiment in Austria." Electoral Studies 35: 242–252.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.electstud.2014.01.010) · Published 2014

Leverages the staggered, non-voluntary repeal of compulsory voting across Austrian provinces to estimate how turnout decline affects party vote shares. Finds large turnout drops have only minimal partisan effects — the main shift is between minor and mainstream parties — challenging strong claims that turnout swings systematically advantage the left.
