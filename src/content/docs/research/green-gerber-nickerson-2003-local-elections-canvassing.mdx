> Green, Donald P., Alan S. Gerber, and David W. Nickerson. 2003. "Getting Out the Vote in Local Elections: Results from Six Door-to-Door Canvassing Experiments." The Journal of Politics 65(4): 1083–1096.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/1468-2508.t01-1-00126) · Published 2003

Six randomized nonpartisan door-to-door canvassing experiments run by student and community coalitions before the November 2001 local elections in Bridgeport, Columbus, Detroit, Minneapolis, Raleigh, and St. Paul. Face-to-face canvassing significantly increased turnout across diverse low-salience local contexts, replicating the New Haven canvassing result.
