> Holbein, John B., and D. Sunshine Hillygus. 2016. "Making Young Voters: The Impact of Preregistration on Youth Turnout." American Journal of Political Science 60(2): 364–382. (See also erratum, AJPS 2017.)

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/ajps.12177) · Published 2016

Uses difference-in-differences and matching on CPS and Florida voter-file data to estimate that teen preregistration laws (allowing registration at 16/17) meaningfully increase youth turnout. Finds effects across subgroups and that preregistrants are not strongly tied to either party. (A 2017 erratum revised some estimates while preserving the positive turnout conclusion.)
