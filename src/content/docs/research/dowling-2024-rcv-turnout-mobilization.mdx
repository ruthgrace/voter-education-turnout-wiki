> Dowling, Eveline, Caroline Tolbert, Nathan K. Micatka, and Todd Donovan. 2024. "Does Ranked Choice Voting Increase Voter Turnout and Mobilization?" Electoral Studies 90: 102816.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.electstud.2024.102816) · Published 2024

Uses national voter-file panel data on off-year local elections plus survey data on campaign contact, comparing RCV and non-RCV jurisdictions (the RCV set includes California/Bay Area cities). Finds voters in RCV jurisdictions are about 17% more likely to turn out in municipal elections and more likely to be contacted by campaigns, suggesting RCV boosts turnout partly via increased mobilization.
