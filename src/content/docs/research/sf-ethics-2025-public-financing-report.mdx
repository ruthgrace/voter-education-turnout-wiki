> San Francisco Ethics Commission. 2025. "San Francisco Public Campaign Financing Program Report for the November 5, 2024 Election." San Francisco, CA (official government report).

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://sfethics.org/ethics/2025/03/san-francisco-public-campaign-financing-program-report-for-the-november-5-2024-election.html) · Published 2025

Official evaluation of San Francisco's 6-to-1 matching-funds public-financing program (matching up to $150 per contributor) for Board of Supervisors and mayoral candidates. Documents program uptake and the shift toward small-donor and public dollars — public funds rose from an average ~33% of candidate spending before the matching-rate increase to ~56% after. (Government program report — not peer-reviewed.)
