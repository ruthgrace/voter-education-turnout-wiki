> Fahey, Kevin, Carol S. Weissert, and Matthew J. Uttermark. 2018. "Extra, Extra, (Don't) Roll-off about It! Newspaper Endorsements for Ballot Measures." State Politics & Policy Quarterly 18(1): 93–113.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1177/1532440018759269) · Published 2018

Analyzes 20 years of Florida ballot measures and local newspaper endorsements. Finds endorsements persuade (associated with ~4.4% fewer "no" votes) but have little effect on roll-off, because the voters who roll off are not the ones consuming newspaper information. A key null: this information channel changes vote direction, not completion.
