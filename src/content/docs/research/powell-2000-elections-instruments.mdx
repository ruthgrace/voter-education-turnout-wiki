> Powell, G. Bingham, Jr. 2000. Elections as Instruments of Democracy: Majoritarian and Proportional Visions. New Haven, CT: Yale University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://yalebooks.yale.edu/book/9780300080162/elections-as-instruments-of-democracy/) · Published 2000

Compares the majoritarian vision (decisive choice between two teams) with the proportional-influence vision (representation for post-election bargaining) across ~150 elections in 20 democracies. Concludes that proportional designs generally produce closer ideological congruence between citizens and policymakers than majoritarian designs. (Scholarly monograph.)
