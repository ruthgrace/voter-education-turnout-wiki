> Golder, Matt, and Jacek Stramski. 2010. "Ideological Congruence and Electoral Institutions." American Journal of Political Science 54(1): 90–106.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-5907.2009.00420.x) · Published 2010

Develops a rigorous conceptualization and measurement of congruence using CSES data. Finds proportional systems produce better citizen–legislature congruence than majoritarian systems, but the advantage largely disappears once one moves from the legislature to actual governments — tempering claims of PR's representational superiority.
