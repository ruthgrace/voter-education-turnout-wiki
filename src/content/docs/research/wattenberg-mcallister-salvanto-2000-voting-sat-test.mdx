> Wattenberg, Martin P., Ian McAllister, and Anthony Salvanto. 2000. "How Voting Is Like Taking an SAT Test: An Analysis of American Voter Rolloff." American Politics Quarterly 28(2): 234–250.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1177/1532673X00028002005) · Published 2000

Observational analysis of roll-off between the presidential and U.S. House races on the same ballots. Finds voters selectively abstain from lower contests primarily because they lack information — "treating voting like a test" and answering only the questions they feel equipped to answer — implicating information deficits rather than pure fatigue.
