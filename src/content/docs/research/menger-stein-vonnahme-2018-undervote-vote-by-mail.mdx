> Menger, Andrew, Robert M. Stein, and Greg Vonnahme. 2018. "Reducing the Undervote With Vote by Mail." American Politics Research 46(6): 1039–1064.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1177/1532673X17737059) · Published 2018

Studies how ballot completion in Colorado changed after the shift to universal vote-by-mail. Finds VBM increases ballot completion, with the effect concentrated in presidential elections. The proposed mechanism is that voting at home gives voters more time and the ability to look up information (guides, etc.) while marking the ballot, creating an information-rich environment that reduces undervoting.
