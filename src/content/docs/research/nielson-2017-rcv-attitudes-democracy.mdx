> Nielson, Lindsay. 2017. "Ranked Choice Voting and Attitudes Toward Democracy in the United States: Results from a Survey Experiment." Politics & Policy 45(4): 535–570.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/polp.12212) · Published 2017

Uses a survey experiment randomly assigning respondents to plurality vs. RCV voting to test effects on satisfaction and democratic confidence. Finds RCV did not meaningfully improve voters' confidence in elections or preference for the system; most participants did not prefer RCV or judge its outcomes as fairer.
