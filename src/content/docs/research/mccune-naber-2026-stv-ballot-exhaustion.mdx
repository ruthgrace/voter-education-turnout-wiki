> McCune, David, and E. E. Naber. 2026. "Ballot Exhaustion in Multiwinner Single Transferable Vote Elections." Working paper, arXiv:2605.12676.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12676) · Published 2026

Empirical analysis of 1,070 Scottish local STV elections (5.4M+ ballots) formalizing types of ballot exhaustion. Finds 27.9% of ballots are exhausted by the final round, but "weight exhaustion" is only 7.1% (most exhausted ballots had already helped elect a candidate); under a proportional completion model only 3.5% of seats change — so raw exhaustion rates substantially overstate lost voter influence under multi-winner STV. (arXiv preprint — not yet peer-reviewed.)
