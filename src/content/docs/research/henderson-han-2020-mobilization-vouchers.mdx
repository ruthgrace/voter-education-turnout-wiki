> Henderson, Geoffrey, and Hahrie Han. 2020. "If We Build It, Only Some Will Come: An Experimental Study of Mobilization for Seattle's Democracy Voucher Program." Journal of Experimental Political Science 9(1): 131–146.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1017/XPS.2020.32) · Published 2020

A field experiment with advocacy partners tested canvassing, texting, digital ads, and email to mobilize voucher use in Seattle's 2017 election. Outreach raised both voucher participation and turnout, but responsiveness was greatest among frequent voters, leading the authors to conclude that transactional mobilization alone is insufficient to engage infrequent participants and meet the program's redistributive goals.
