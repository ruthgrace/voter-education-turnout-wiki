> Garzia, Diego, and Stefan Marschall. 2012. "Voting Advice Applications Under Review: The State of Research." International Journal of Electronic Governance 5(3/4): 203–222.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1504/IJEG.2012.051309) · Published 2012

Reviews and maps the research literature on voting advice applications (VAAs) — online tools that match users' issue positions to parties/candidates — and sets out a comparative research agenda. Synthesizes evidence on why VAAs spread across Europe and their emerging effects on political knowledge, turnout, and vote choice.
