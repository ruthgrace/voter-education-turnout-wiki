> Benadè, Gerdus, Ruth Buck, Moon Duchin, Dara Gold, and Thomas Weighill. 2023. "Ranked Choice Voting and Proportional Representation." Political Analysis 31(3): 305–324.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://ssrn.com/abstract=3778021) · Published 2023

Builds a statistical/computational toolkit (incorporating voter-bloc cohesion) to compare how reliably electoral systems secure representation for people of color, using real and modeled US elections. Finds multi-winner STV ("proportional RCV") stably and reliably secures minority representation, whereas single-member-district plurality performance is highly variable across turnout and demographic shifts.
