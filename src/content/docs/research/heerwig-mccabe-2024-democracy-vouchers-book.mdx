> Heerwig, Jennifer A., and Brian J. McCabe. 2024. Democracy Vouchers and the Promise of Fairer Elections in Seattle. Philadelphia: Temple University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://tupress.temple.edu/books/democracy-vouchers-and-the-promise-of-fairer-elections-in-seattle) · Published 2024

A book-length synthesis of six-plus years of research on Seattle's voucher program's effects on the donor and candidate pools and on representational inequality. Reports that low-income residents and people of color were more likely to use vouchers than to give cash, and that the candidate pool became more diverse in age, race, and gender, while examining legal challenges and independent expenditures. (Peer-reviewed academic monograph.)
