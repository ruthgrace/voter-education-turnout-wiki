> Grumbach, Jacob M., and Charlotte Hill. 2022. "Rock the Registration: Same Day Registration Increases Turnout of Young Voters." The Journal of Politics 84(1): 405–417.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1086/714776) · Published 2022

Using a difference-in-differences design across states adopting same-day registration (SDR), estimates registration-reform effects by age. Finds SDR increases turnout among 18–24-year-olds in presidential elections by roughly 3–7 percentage points, a substantially larger effect than for older voters, indicating registration barriers disproportionately depress youth participation.
