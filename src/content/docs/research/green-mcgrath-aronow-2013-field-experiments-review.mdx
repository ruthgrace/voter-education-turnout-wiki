> Green, Donald P., Mary C. McGrath, and Peter M. Aronow. 2013. "Field Experiments and the Study of Voter Turnout." Journal of Elections, Public Opinion and Parties 23(1): 27–48.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1080/17457289.2012.728223) · Published 2013

A synthesizing review and meta-analytic summary of the GOTV field-experiment literature, reporting average treatment effects by contact mode (door-to-door canvassing, direct mail, robocalls/live calls, and text messages). It confirms canvassing and personal contact as most effective, mail and calls as modest, and organizes the field into tactic-testing versus social-psychological-theory-testing strands.
