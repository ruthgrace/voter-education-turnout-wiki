> Wolk, Sara, Jameson Quinn, and Mark Ogren. 2023. "STAR Voting, Equality of Voice, and Voter Satisfaction: Considerations for Voting Method Reform." Constitutional Political Economy 34(3): 310–334.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10602-022-09389-3) · Published 2023

Introduces STAR (Score Then Automatic Runoff) and evaluates it against plurality and IRV using simulation metrics — Voter Satisfaction Efficiency (VSE) and strategic-incentive measures — across spatial electorates. Reports STAR achieves high VSE comparable to Condorcet methods and resists center-squeeze and strategic voting. Note: the authors are affiliated with the Equal Vote Coalition (STAR's advocacy organization); this is peer-reviewed but advocacy-adjacent, simulation-based work — essentially the only peer-reviewed article specifically on STAR.
