> Brennan, Jason, and Lisa Hill. 2014. Compulsory Voting: For and Against. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1017/CBO9781139649742) · Published 2014

A structured debate book: Brennan argues compulsory voting is an unjust violation of liberty that may worsen decision quality, while Hill defends it as a reasonable imposition that produces more legitimate, representative high-turnout elections. The definitive normative treatment of the mandatory-voting debate.
