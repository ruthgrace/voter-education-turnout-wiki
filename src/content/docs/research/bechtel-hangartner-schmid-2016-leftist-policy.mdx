> Bechtel, Michael M., Dominik Hangartner, and Lukas Schmid. 2016. "Does Compulsory Voting Increase Support for Leftist Policy?" American Journal of Political Science 60(3): 752–767.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/ajps.12224) · Published 2016

Exploits sanctioned compulsory voting in the Swiss canton of Vaud (1900–1970), finding it raised turnout in federal referendums by ~30 points. The mobilization of lower-income citizens translated into up to 20 points greater electoral support for leftist policy positions.
