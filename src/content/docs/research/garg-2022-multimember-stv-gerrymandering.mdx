> Garg, Nikhil, Wes Gurnee, David Rothschild, and David Shmoys. 2022. "Combatting Gerrymandering with Ranked Choice Voting: An Experimental Analysis of Multi-member Districts." (ACM EC 2022; journal version, Operations Research.) Working paper arXiv:2107.07083.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.07083) · Published 2022

A simulation/experimental study of three-member STV districts across all US states, testing whether multi-member RCV curtails gerrymandering. Finds that with three-member STV districts, fairness-minded commissions can achieve proportional outcomes in every state while sharply reducing partisan actors' ability to gerrymander, robust to cross-party voting. (Conference-refereed; linked record is the working-paper form.)
