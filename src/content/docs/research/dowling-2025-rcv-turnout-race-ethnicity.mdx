> Dowling, Eveline, Caroline Tolbert, Nathan K. Micatka, and Todd Donovan. 2025. "Is Ranked-Choice Voting Associated With Turnout Across Race/Ethnic Groups?" Social Science Quarterly 106 (online first).

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/ssqu.70025) · Published 2025

Uses Catalist-enhanced voter-file data comparing odd-year local elections in RCV versus non-RCV places (plus within-Minnesota comparisons) to ask whether RCV depresses turnout among voters of color. Finds RCV is not associated with lower turnout for people of color; if anything, each race/ethnic group examined had higher turnout in RCV cities, countering claims that RCV widens racial participation gaps. (Online first.)
