> Blais, André, and Marc André Bodet. 2006. "Does Proportional Representation Foster Closer Congruence Between Citizens and Policy Makers?" Comparative Political Studies 39(10): 1243–1262.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1177/0010414005284374) · Published 2006

Uses CSES left-right self-placements versus cabinet-party positions across 31 election studies. Argues PR has two offsetting effects — more (less centrist) parties increase voter-party distance, but more centrist coalition governments decrease government-voter distance — so the net effect of PR on citizen–government congruence is essentially nil, challenging Powell's optimistic reading.
