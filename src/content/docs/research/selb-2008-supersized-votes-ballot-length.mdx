> Selb, Peter. 2008. "Supersized Votes: Ballot Length, Uncertainty, and Choice in Direct Legislation Elections." Public Choice 135(3): 319–336.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11127-007-9265-7) · Published 2008

Cross-national analysis of direct-legislation ballots (notably Switzerland, where voters routinely face many simultaneous measures). Finds that longer ballots increase voter uncertainty and abstention on individual propositions, providing comparative evidence that ballot length/complexity drives measure-level undervoting beyond the U.S. context.
