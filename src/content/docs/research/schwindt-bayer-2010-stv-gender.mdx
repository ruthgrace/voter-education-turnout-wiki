> Schwindt-Bayer, Leslie A., Michael Malecki, and Brian F. Crisp. 2010. "Candidate Gender and Electoral Success in Single Transferable Vote Systems." British Journal of Political Science 40(3): 693–709.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1017/S000712340999010X) · Published 2010

Multilevel analysis of candidate-level outcomes under STV in Australia, Ireland, and Malta, testing whether women are disadvantaged by preferential voting. Finds the preferential, candidate-centered structure of STV does not systematically penalize women candidates once party and district factors are accounted for.
