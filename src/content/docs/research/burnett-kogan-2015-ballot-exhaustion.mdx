> Burnett, Craig M., and Vladimir Kogan. 2015. "Ballot (and Voter) 'Exhaustion' Under Instant Runoff Voting: An Examination of Four Ranked-Choice Elections." Electoral Studies 37: 41–49.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.electstud.2014.11.006) · Published 2015

Analyzes images of more than 600,000 ballots across four local ranked-choice elections (San Francisco and Bay Area contests) to measure ballot exhaustion. Finds exhaustion rates of 9.6%–27.1%, meaning the eventual winner in all four cases won with less than a majority of ballots originally cast — challenging the majoritarian claim made for IRV.
