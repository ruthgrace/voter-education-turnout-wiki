> Neely, Francis, and Jason McDaniel. 2015. "Overvoting and the Equality of Voice under Instant-Runoff Voting in San Francisco." California Journal of Politics and Policy 7(4): 1–27.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://escholarship.org/uc/item/8tm3s6hz) · Published 2015

Examines San Francisco elections since IRV adoption (2004) using ballot and precinct data to test whether overvoting and declining ranking erode equality of voice. Finds no evidence that candidate ranking is in decline, and that although overvotes are more common under IRV than plurality, the errors appear driven by general ballot complexity rather than IRV per se.
