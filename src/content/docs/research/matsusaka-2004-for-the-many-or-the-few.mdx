> Matsusaka, John G. 2004. For the Many or the Few: The Initiative, Public Policy, and American Democracy. Chicago: University of Chicago Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/book/chicago/F/bo3615566.html) · Published 2004

Analyzes a century of state and local initiative use (with California central) against opinion and fiscal-policy data. Concludes the initiative process systematically shifts tax and spending policy toward positions supported by majorities, rebutting the claim that direct democracy is captured by wealthy special interests — and bearing on debates over voter competence in proposition voting.
