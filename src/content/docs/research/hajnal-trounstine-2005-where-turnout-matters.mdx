> Hajnal, Zoltan L., and Jessica Trounstine. 2005. "Where Turnout Matters: The Consequences of Uneven Turnout in City Politics." The Journal of Politics 67(2): 515–535.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1468-2508.2005.00327.x) · Published 2005

Arguing that national-election studies understate turnout bias, examines local elections and finds that low and uneven turnout substantially reduces the representation of Latinos and Asian Americans on city councils and in mayoralties. Shows that who votes can meaningfully change who governs where minority populations are pivotal.
