> Kosar, Kevin R., Jaehun Lee, and Jack Santucci. 2025. An Initial Assessment of Proportional Ranked-Choice Voting in Portland, Oregon. Washington, DC: American Enterprise Institute (report).

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://www.aei.org/research-products/report/an-initial-assessment-of-proportional-ranked-choice-voting-in-portland-oregon/) · Published 2025

Assesses Portland's first (Nov 2024) proportional-RCV city-council elections. Finds no meaningful turnout increase (roll-off from federal to local races unchanged), voters frequently ranked all six candidates but rankings had little effect on outcomes, and the elected council was demographically diverse (Asian, Black, and Latino members meeting or exceeding population shares). (Think-tank report — not peer-reviewed.)
