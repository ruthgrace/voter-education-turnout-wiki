> Achen, Christopher H., and Larry M. Bartels. 2016. Democracy for Realists: Why Elections Do Not Produce Responsive Government. Princeton, NJ: Princeton University Press.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://press.princeton.edu/books/hardcover/9780691169446/democracy-for-realists) · Published 2016

Attacks the "folk theory" of democracy, arguing that voters (even informed ones) choose based on social identities and partisan loyalties rather than issues or retrospective performance, and that they rationalize facts to fit those loyalties. Directly critiques the "miracle of aggregation" and the idea that information or cues rescue collective competence.
