> Center for Information and Research on Civic Learning and Engagement (CIRCLE), Tisch College, Tufts University. 2022. Growing Voters: Building Institutions and Community Ecosystems for Equitable Election Participation. Medford, MA: Tufts University.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://circle.tufts.edu/latest-research/growing-voters-engaging-youth-they-reach-voting-age-strengthen-democracy) · Published 2022

CIRCLE's flagship report introduces the "Growing Voters" framework, arguing that youth turnout depends on engaging young people (including pre-voting-age) across schools, families, communities, and election institutions. Documents that mandatory, well-supported K–12 civics and school-based voter registration boost civic knowledge and engagement without producing partisanship.
