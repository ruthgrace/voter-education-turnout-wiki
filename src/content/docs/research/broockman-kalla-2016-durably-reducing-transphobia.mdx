> Broockman, David, and Joshua Kalla. 2016. "Durably Reducing Transphobia: A Field Experiment on Door-to-Door Canvassing." Science 352(6282): 220–224.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aad9713) · Published 2016

In a randomized field experiment, 56 canvassers held ~10-minute perspective-taking ("deep canvassing") conversations with 501 voters in South Florida about transgender rights. A single conversation markedly reduced anti-transgender prejudice, with effects persisting at least three months and exceeding the average decline in US homophobia from 1998–2012.
