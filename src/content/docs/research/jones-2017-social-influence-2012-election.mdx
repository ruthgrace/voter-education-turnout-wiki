> Jones, Jason J., Robert M. Bond, Eytan Bakshy, Dean Eckles, and James H. Fowler. 2017. "Social Influence and Political Mobilization: Further Evidence From a Randomized Experiment in the 2012 U.S. Presidential Election." PLOS ONE 12(4): e0173851.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0173851) · Published 2017

A replication of the Facebook mobilization experiment during the higher-salience 2012 presidential election. Despite the expectation of smaller effects in high-stakes elections, the social message significantly increased voting among recipients and also increased turnout among their close friends, with the total spillover effect likely larger than the direct effect.
