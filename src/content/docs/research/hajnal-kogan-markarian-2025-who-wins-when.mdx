> Hajnal, Zoltan L., Vladimir Kogan, and G. Agustin Markarian. 2025. "Who Wins When? Election Timing and Descriptive Representation." American Journal of Political Science (early view). DOI: 10.1111/ajps.12930.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/ajps.12930) · Published 2025

Extends the timing research from who votes to who wins, using California cities that shifted to even-year on-cycle elections. Finds on-cycle timing increases the election of candidates of color, with the compositional turnout changes sharply reducing (in some specifications eliminating) Latino underrepresentation on city councils. (Early view; volume/issue/pages pending.)
