> Yu, Jinhai. 2019. "Does State Online Voter Registration Increase Voter Turnout?" Social Science Quarterly 100(3): 620–634.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/ssqu.12598) · Published 2019

Uses CPS data (2000–2014) with difference-in-differences and individual-level instrumental-variable analysis to estimate the turnout effect of states adopting online voter registration (a reform California implemented in 2012). Finds OVR raises turnout, concentrated among young voters (about a 3-point increase in presidential years), with negligible effects for the general population.
