> Vanderleeuw, James M., and Richard L. Engstrom. 1987. "Race, Referendums, and Roll-Off." The Journal of Politics 49(4): 1081–1092.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.2307/2130785) · Published 1987

Examines roll-off on referendum/ballot questions and its relationship to voter race and ballot placement. Finds roll-off is substantially higher on down-ballot referendum items than on candidate races and varies systematically across groups — an early, foundational demonstration that ballot measures are the locus of greatest undervoting.
