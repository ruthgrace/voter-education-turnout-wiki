> Nickerson, David W. 2007. "Quality Is Job One: Professional and Volunteer Voter Mobilization Calls." American Journal of Political Science 51(2): 269–282.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1540-5907.2007.00250.x) · Published 2007

Directly compares professional (commercial) and volunteer phone banks in randomized experiments, isolating call quality as the key variable. Unhurried, personal, high-quality calls raise turnout regardless of caller type, explaining why perfunctory commercial calls fail while engaged volunteer calls succeed.
