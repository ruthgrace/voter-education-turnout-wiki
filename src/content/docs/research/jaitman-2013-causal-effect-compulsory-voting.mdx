> Jaitman, Laura. 2013. "The Causal Effect of Compulsory Voting Laws on Turnout: Does Skill Matter?" Journal of Economic Behavior & Organization 92: 79–93.

**Source:** [View publication ↗](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jebo.2013.04.014) · Published 2013

Uses an age-based natural experiment to estimate compulsory voting's causal turnout effect, finding it raises turnout by ~18 points (28%), with the increase roughly twice as large for unskilled as for skilled citizens. Directly demonstrates that compulsory voting narrows the socioeconomic/skill gap in participation.
